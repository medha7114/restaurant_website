Our Signature Dishes

Here you can view the signature dishes of our restaurant

Masor Tenga

Masor Tenga also called Assamese Fish Curry tangy and light fish curry from Assam, often made with tomatoes and lemon for a fresh and sour flavor.

Matar Pulcha

Matar Pulcha is a spicy, aromatic green pea curry, perfect for pairing with roti.

Malai Kofta

Malai Kofta is a creamy North Indian dish with fried paneer and potato dumplings in a tomato-based gravy.


Thalassery Biriyani

A fragrant Malabar dish featuring tender chicken or mutton, aromatic rice, unique spices, and garnished with fried onions, raisins, and cashews.

Maharastrian Thali

Maharashtrian Thali includes bhakri or chapati, rice, dal, vegetable bhaji, usal, batata vada, pickles, papad, and desserts like puran poli or shrikhand.

Cheesy Kunafa

Cheesy Kunafa is a Middle Eastern dessert with shredded phyllo pastry, creamy cheese, and sweet syrup.
